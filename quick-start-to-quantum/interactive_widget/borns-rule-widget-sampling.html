Interactive Bloch Sphere (Born's Rule)

Visualize the qubit state on the Bloch sphere and explore Born's rule with measurement probabilities and sampling.

Instructions

  1. Adjust Theta (θ) and Phi (φ) sliders to set the qubit state on the Bloch sphere. Tab to move between sliders; the focused slider shows a green focus ring.
  2. Toggle Measure to switch between state view and sampling: click the measure buttons to see outcomes and the histogram.
  3. Bloch sphere: Click or Tab to focus the sphere; then drag to rotate, or use Arrow keys to rotate the view. A green outline shows when the sphere has keyboard focus.

Keyboard: Tab to move between sliders and the Bloch sphere. Arrow keys rotate the sphere when it is focused; Enter/Space activate buttons.
