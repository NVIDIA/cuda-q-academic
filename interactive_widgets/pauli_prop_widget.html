Pauli Propagation

Simulate the backward flow and branching of observables through quantum circuits.

Instructions

  1. Select a circuit (Ex 1 or Ex 2). Click the observable buttons (X, Y, Z) for each qubit to set the initial observable.
  2. Watch the Propagation History: observables are pushed backward through the circuit with Heisenberg rules (H, S, CNOT, RX).
  3. The Expectation Value is the sum of coefficients for branches containing only I or Z.

Keyboard: ESC to reset. Enter/Space to activate buttons.

Heisenberg Rules

Clifford Gates (Backward):
Hadamard (H): $Z \leftrightarrow X, Y \to -Y$
Phase (S): $X \to Y, Y \to -X$
Rotation (RX): Branching logic
$Z \to \cos\theta Z + \sin\theta Y$
$Y \to \cos\theta Y - \sin\theta Z$
$X \to X$ (Identity)
