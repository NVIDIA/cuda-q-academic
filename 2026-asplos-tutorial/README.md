# AI for Quantum Computing

Artificial Intelligence is emerging as a powerful and essential tool for enabling the most challenging aspects of hybrid quantum-classical computing. As quantum systems scale, they face immense systems-level bottlenecks in compilation, quantum error correction (QEC), and optimization, all of which are deeply intertwined with hardware constraints and architectural limitations. This half-day, interactive tutorial educates the community on this emerging intersection, providing hands-on experience in applying cutting-edge AI techniques to solve these critical problems. Using CUDA-Q, an open-source, qubit-agnostic platform designed for hybrid quantum-classical workflows, participants will explore how AI models can navigate the trade-offs in compiling, executing, and securing quantum applications.
