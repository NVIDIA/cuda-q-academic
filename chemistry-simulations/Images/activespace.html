NH₃ Active Space Visualizer

Interactive orbital diagram for Ammonia (NH₃) using the 6-31G Basis Set.

Instructions

  1. Standard MO diagram: Each horizontal line is an orbital; ↑ = spin up, ↓ = spin down. Colored boxes show Core, Active, and Virtual regions from your selection.
  2. Set Active Space Electrons (0–10, even number) and Active Space Orbitals (0–15) using the inputs above.
  3. The diagram updates automatically. Core orbitals (blue) are always filled; active (green) and virtual (red) depend on your choices.
  4. Ensure electrons ≤ 2×orbitals in the active space and that the total configuration fits within 10 electrons and 15 orbitals.

Keyboard shortcuts: Press ESC to reset to default (4 electrons, 4 orbitals). Use Tab to move between inputs; press Enter after typing to confirm.
