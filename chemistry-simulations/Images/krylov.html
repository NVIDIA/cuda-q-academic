Eigenvalue Subspace Analysis

Explore Krylov-style subspace diagonalization by selecting a sub-matrix block and comparing its eigenvalues to the full matrix.

Instructions

  1. Set Subspace Size (m×m) with the slider and choose the Subspace Corner (top-left of the block).
  2. The matrix view highlights the selected block. Eigenvalues and trace are shown below.

Keyboard: ESC to reset. Enter/Space to activate controls.

Note: This widget is part of the CUDA-Q Academic lesson entitled "Krylov Subspace Diagonalization (Chemistry Simulations)". You can learn more by visiting here.
