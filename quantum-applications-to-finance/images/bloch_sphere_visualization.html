Bloch Sphere Visualization

Visualize the circuit's output on the Bloch Sphere by entering rotation angles for each gate.

Instructions

  1. Type rotation angles (in radians, 0 – 6.28) for Rx, Ry, and Rz into the input fields. Use Tab to move between fields.
  2. Watch the circuit diagram and the Bloch sphere update in real time as you type.
  3. Bloch sphere: Click or Tab to focus the sphere window; then drag to rotate the view, or use Arrow keys to rotate. A green outline shows when the sphere has keyboard focus.

Keyboard: Tab to move between inputs and the Bloch sphere. Arrow keys rotate the sphere when it is focused; Enter/Space activate buttons.
