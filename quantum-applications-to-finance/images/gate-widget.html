Quantum Gate Visualization

Click the gates to build a circuit and see the state evolve on the Bloch Sphere.

Instructions

  1. Click a gate (X, Y, Z, H, S, T) to add it to the circuit, or Tab to a gate and press Enter/Space. Use Arrow Left/Right to move between gate buttons.
  2. Use Backstep to remove the last gate, or Reset to clear the circuit.
  3. Bloch sphere: Click or Tab to focus it; then drag to rotate, or use Arrow keys to rotate the view. A green outline shows when the sphere has focus.

Keyboard: Tab to move between gate buttons and the Bloch sphere. Arrow Left/Right move between gates when a gate is focused; Arrow keys rotate the sphere when it is focused. Enter/Space activate the focused button. ESC to reset.
